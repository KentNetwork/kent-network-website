Kent.Network is a multi-initiative project that aims to catapult Kent into the 21st century.

A wireless network, free for all We provide a network, with coverage across Kent, that allows you to securely send and receive data from remote devices.

Local monitoring = Local services We provide access to a range of sensors, installed throughout the county, to help raise awareness about the state of the world around us.

Open data to promote citizen engagement We collect, archive and provide access to data to enable local insight, engagement and decision making.
